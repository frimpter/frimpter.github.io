Temperature Analysis


The analysis of temperature showed expected results, with higher temperatures for cities closer to the Equator and for those in the Southern Hemisphere which was enjoying Summer on March 12th. As expected, temperatures in the Northern Hemisphere dropped for cities closer to the Arctic Circle, indicated by the light blue line on the right of the graph.

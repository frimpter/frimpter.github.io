Wind Speed Analysis


The analysis of wind speed was generally unremarkable. The day of analysis did not show any particularly high wind speeds from the entire sample of cities, with the max wind speed about 30 mph on the day. Cities in the Northern Hemisphere, which were represented more in the analysis overall, had typically low wind speeds, with no outstanding trends based on latitude except that those with the higher wind speeds tended to be roughly mid-way between the Tropic of Cancer and Arctic Circle.

Humidity Analysis


The analysis of humidity showed a small cluster of Southern Hemisphere (Summer) cities with maximum humidity around the Equator, as marked by the central red dashed line. In the Northern Hemisphere, humidity was high overall on the day of the analysis, with most city clusters >60% humidity on the day.
